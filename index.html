About StayEasy

StayEasy was born from my own frustrating experiences while traveling. During a trip across East Africa, I found myself struggling with disconnected booking systems, unreliable information, and communication barriers with local hotels.

The idea struck me: what if there was a seamless platform that connected travelers with hotels while providing both parties with the tools they needed for a smooth experience? This vision led to the creation of StayEasy.

This project is part of my portfolio for the ALX Software Engineering Program, developed over a six-week period. It demonstrates my skills in full-stack development while solving a real-world problem in the travel industry.
